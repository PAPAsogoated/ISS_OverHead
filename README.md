# ISS OVERHEAD

**This is an app that connects to the the iss satellite s coordinates data from the verified website and it checks if the satellite is close to your coordinate, if the satellite is in range it sends your specified email a mail that informs you and tells you to look up !**

## How to use:

To use the app first enter your own latitude and longitude in the main.py file where i specified for you:

![lat_lng](<SC's/lat_lng.png>)


After entering the coordinates you need to enter your email servers SMTP address to be able to use the app (if you don't know the smtp address check this website: https://serversmtp.com/outgoing-smtp-mail-server/), then you need to enter the target email and the message and your all set.

![mail](<SC's/mail.png>)

enter you SMTP address in the line that is defining "connection", enter it inside the double quotation marks.
